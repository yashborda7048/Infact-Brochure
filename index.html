The problem
Lenders face substantial risks due to traditional credit data being incomplete, outdated and inaccurate.
The solution
A platform that offers complete, real-time credit data to lenders, enabling them to make faster, more informed decisions.

Current challenges with CRA data

Outdated credit data

Credit information and affordability data is by default out-of-date. As a result of this, consumers and lenders are missing a key component in enabling access to responsible and sustainable credit.

Inaccurate matching

The FCA Credit Information Market Study highlights significant differences and misrepresentations in credit information across the 3 large CRAs. For example, they represent 100M consumer identities – far beyond the actual lendable consumers market.

Disadvantaging lenders and consumers

  • Affordability assessments use outdated data

  • No protection against loan stacking

  • Alternative contextual data cannot be effectively ingested into the system

  • Difficulties in matching new credit information that leads to poor outcomes

  • Non-PCA lenders are locked out of CATO data

  • Promote credit scores which are unfair on consumers
